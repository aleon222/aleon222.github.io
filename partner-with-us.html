Partner With Us

Types of Collaboration

All partnerships are rooted in mutual goals, shared values, and collaborative efforts.

Culturally Competent Mental Health Services:

  • Collaborate on designing and delivering services that prioritize cultural sensitivity.
  • Develop tools tailored to the unique mental health needs of Black women.

Educational Workshops and Training:

  • Partner to create workshops that address mental health challenges in the Black community.
  • Combine expertise to promote mental health literacy and self-care practices.

Advocacy and Awareness Campaigns:

  • Pool efforts to raise awareness about mental health disparities and advocate for equitable care.
  • Work together on campaigns that challenge stigma and encourage open dialogue.

Community Engagement:

  • We co-create events, support groups, and virtual spaces for community building.
  • Engage directly with Black women to address their mental health needs in a supportive environment.

Resource Exchange:

  • Share toolkits and educational materials.
  • Jointly develop content reflecting the diverse experiences of Black women.

Resource Pooling:

  • Share uses of venues, platforms, and materials for co-hosted events and seminars.

Additional Partnership Models:

  • Skill Exchange: Partner for mutual upskilling, such as one organization providing training in return for specialized services.
  • Volunteer Exchange: Share volunteer support to enhance activities at both organizations.
  • Data Sharing and Research: Collaborate on data collection and analysis for shared causes.
  • Policy and Advocacy Alliances: Join forces to advocate for changes or raise awareness about systemic issues.

Media & Content Sharing:

  • Collaborate on social media campaigns, blogs, and webinars.
  • Feature each other's achievements in newsletters and success stories.

Commitment to Community:

  • Joint work on research and publications.
  • We offer mutual support in achieving each organization's community-oriented goals.

Financial Collaboration:

  • Donations and Grants: Contribute through one-time or recurring contributions, sponsor specific programs, or collaborate to apply for grants that align with our shared missions.


Benefits of Collaboration:

  • Amplified impact through shared resources and combined efforts.
  • Increased visibility and credibility for both organizations.
  • Access to specialized expertise in culturally sensitive mental health support.
  • Our collaboration strengthens networks within the mental health advocacy community.
