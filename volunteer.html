Get Involved with Reecee’s Reach

Flexible Roles: Adjust your level of involvement based on your availability and interests.

Training 7 Mentorship: Develop vital skills in mental health support, community organization, and more.

Note: All volunteers must believe in the mission of Reecee's Reach and work well with people of diverse backgrounds with mental health challenges.

Volunteer Coordinator

  • Will establish, oversee, and coordinate volunteer and service opportunities for Reecee's Reach.
  • Experience hosting volunteer groups is a plus.
  • Email volunteer@reeceesreach.org to apply.

General Opportunities

Make a Difference:

  • Directly impact lives through mental health advocacy and education.
  • Be part of a community breaking down barriers and destigmatizing mental health.

Personal & Professional Growth:

  • Gain skills in community organization, outreach, and public speaking.
  • Networking opportunities within a passionate and diverse community.

What You Can Do:

  • Participate in mental health workshops and seminars.
  • Provide peer support under expert guidance.
  • Help in administrative tasks and campaign management.

Join Us:

  • Flexible hours and roles to suit your skills and commitments.
  • Ongoing training and mentorship opportunities.

Specialized Volunteer Opportunities

Reecee's Reach Ambassador:

  • Serve as a liaison between Reecee's Reach and the community.
  • Engage in outreach and educational initiatives.
  • Actively promote Reecee's Reach's events, programs, and services.

Reecee's Reach Online Social Media Ambassadors:

  • Amplify our message by sharing our content on social media.
  • Engage with the online community to spread awareness about mental health.
  • Create and share your posts promoting Reecee's Reach using our designated hashtags.

Volunteers for Sisters of Strength (SOS):

  • Peer Counseling: Provide empathetic listening and emotional support under expert guidance.
  • Companion Services: Build social connections with clients, offering emotional support and company.
  • Home Support: Assist clients with daily tasks to maintain a safe living environment.
  • Resource Assistance: Guide individuals through medical, legal, and financial challenges.
  • Education & Outreach: Engage in mental health education through workshops, seminars, and campaigns.

Volunteers for MLK Day of Service Feminine Hygiene Drive:

  • Collection & Sorting: Gather, categorize, and prepare period products for distribution.
  • Community Distribution: Collaborate with mutual aid organizations to distribute collected items.
  • Public Awareness: Foster conversations and educational activities related to mental health during the drive.
