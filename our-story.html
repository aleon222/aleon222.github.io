Our Mission

At Reecee’s Reach, we envision a world where Black women can access culturally competent, Christ-centered, and affordable mental health care. This fosters a journey of healing, empowerment, and dignity.

Reecee’s Reach is a Christ-centered nonprofit committed to nurturing the well-being of Black women. We provide support and culturally competent care for mental health challenges, mental illness, substance use, co-occurring disorders, and the journey toward mental health recovery.
