Our Mission

Reecee's Reach is dedicated to transforming mental health care for Black women by providing culturally sensitive care that ensures everyone feels heard, seen, and understood.

Reecee's Reach is a Christ-centered nonprofit devoted to nurturing the well-being of Black women by providing a supportive environment and culturally competent care for mental health challenges, mental illness, substance use, co-occurring disorders, and the journey toward mental health recovery.


Reecee's Reach, a national nonprofit based in Birmingham, Alabama, empowers Black women to thrive through culturally competent mental health care.

Disclaimer: Reecee's Reach focuses on supporting the mental health of Black women through culturally competent care, addressing challenges like mental illness, substance use, and co-occurring disorders. While our services are tailored for Black women, we welcome and serve individuals of all races and genders without discrimination.
