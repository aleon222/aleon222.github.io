SoS: Sisters of Strength

Sisters of Strength (SOS) is Reecee's Reach flagship program, set to launch in 2024.

This innovative initiative is committed to providing comprehensive mental health services specifically for Black women, introducing a transformative approach to mental health support that focuses on culturally sensitive counseling, community support, and resources.


The cornerstone of the SOS program is its focus on various services catering to the unique experiences and needs of the women we serve. We offer peer counseling, which will provide essential emotional support, create a safe space for sharing experiences, and offer strategies for managing mental health challenges.

Our Companion Services and Home Support programs aim to enhance our clientele's social connectivity and overall well-being, providing emotional support and assistance with daily tasks to promote independence and ensure a safe, comfortable living environment.

The SOS program recognizes that mental health often intersects with medical, legal, and financial issues. To address this, we offer assistance services in these areas, helping individuals navigate health and legal systems and manage their finances.

Our Comprehensive Support Services intervene during the darkest times when individuals struggle with daily activities due to mental health challenges, providing a critical lifeline and helping them regain control and functionality.
