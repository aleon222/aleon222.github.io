The Adinkra Symbols used across this website belong to the Akan people of present day Ghana. The Adinkra symbols featured are Obaatan Awaamu, Akoma, Nyame Nti, Nkonsonkonson. These symbols are used to visually represent the values of Reecee’s Reecee: Compassion, Community and Faith. Learn more about Adinkra symbols of Ghana here.
